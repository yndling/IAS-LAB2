# Conclusion

In this lab, we tested different encryption methods and saw that TLS 1.3 keeps our data safe - Wireshark can see the handshake but NOT our secret messages! We also learned that while traditional crypto like ECDHE works great now, quantum computers could break it someday, so post-quantum algorithms like Kyber and NTRU are the future solution. Basically: encryption protects us today, and post-quantum crypto will protect us tomorrow!
